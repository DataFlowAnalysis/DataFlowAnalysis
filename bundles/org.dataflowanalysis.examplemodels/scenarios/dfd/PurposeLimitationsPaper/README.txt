These Diagrams are from the Paper 'Precise Analysis of Purpose Limitation in Data Flow Diagrams' by Alshareef et al.

The Model Presented without violations is located under NoViolation.json.
GuestViolation and StorageConstraintViolation are two violations introduced in the Paper.

ExampleDFDs and ExampleDFDCombineAndSplit contain examples for small DataFlowDiagrams presented in the paper. They are not linked to the main Model presented in the paper.
